About us

The Country Thistle has been a fixture in Pleasant Valley, New York, for more than 25 years. Four years ago the original owners retired; and the store was purchased by Sue Holland, a Pleasant Valley resident.

Since her start in March 2008, Sue has taken this Pleasant Valley shopping "landmark" to the next level of what a friendly, local shopping experience should be: customer-oriented, pleasant, personal, and always a step ahead of the ordinary.

She offers shoppers personal service, great prices, and free gift wrapping all year round!
